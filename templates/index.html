Enhanced Chest X-Ray Classification

Upload an Chest X-Ray image to classify it as normal or identify tumor types

About the Project

This web application uses a deep learning model (ResNet50V2) trained on a dataset of MRI brain images to classify them into four categories:

  • COVID-19: Signs of viral pneumonia, ground-glass opacities, or bilateral lung infiltrates.
  • NORMAL: No abnormalities detected in the X-ray
  • PNEUMONIA: Consolidation, lobar infiltrates, or other signs of lung infection
  • TUBERCULOSIS: Cavitary lesions, hilar lymphadenopathy, or miliary patterns

The model was trained to ensure high performance even on low-quality images, promoting excellent generalization for diverse healthcare settings.
